Step 1:

Sit down in a quiet space where you wont be disturbed easily. Start by conscienly taking a few long and deep breaths. This will help you release tension in your body. When you feel your body slowly releasing the excess tension, slowly start with taking "normal" breaths (try not to force your breathing).

Step 2:

Lift your awareness to points in your body that feel "unnatural". If this is in your back, slowly lift your back to a comfortable but active position. If this is your chest, try to relax your chest to a comfortable position. What body part it may be, dont force them into a position but try to keep a active awareness of them.

Step 3:

Now that your body feels present, raise your awareness to your breath again. If your mind wanders, kindly bring yourself back to your breath. This process will happen often but that is okay, just bring the focus back to your breathing. Repeat this process for as long as you like.

Step 4:

When you feel like you are ready, take a few deep breaths again and kindly shift your awareness to the sounds of your enviroment. This could be cars driving by or even the stilness of the enviroment you're in. This is to shift your concentration for you to the outside world again. Now slowly open your eyes and take a few deep breaths again.
