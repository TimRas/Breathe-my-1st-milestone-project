Step 1:

Start off be finding a calm spot where you wont be disturbed easily. Take a few deep breaths to release straining tension in your body. You can take as much breaths and time as you want to feel comfortable in the position you're in. Close your eyes and feel the ease in your body.

Step 2:

Imagine yourself experiencing complete physical and emotional well-being and inner peace. Imagine feeling perfect love for yourself and thanking yourself for being who you are. You are perfect the way you are and so you deserve all the love from yourself. This practice might feel a little uncomfortable in the beginning but with practice this will become natural.

Step 3:

Repeat three or four positive, reassuring phrases to yourself. This could be: "May I be happy", "May I welcome love in my life" or "May I be peaceful today" These phrases help you affirm positive feelings and sensations in your awareness. These might feel a little uncomfortable aswell but rest assured, with times this will be second nature.

Step 4:

Let these feelings and sensation of love and positivity run through your body and mind. Stick with them for as long as you like. Once you feel that you are ready, Thank yourself for the comfort and love that you presented yourself. Slowly open your eyes and cross your arms to give yourself a nice comforting hug.
